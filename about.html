About EcoDispose

EcoDispose is your trusted guide to finding the nearest e-waste drop-off centers in Patna. Our mission is to promote safe, responsible disposal of electronic waste through transparency, awareness, and technology.

Why We Exist

Every year, tons of electronic waste end up in landfills causing long-term environmental damage. EcoDispose aims to change that by:

  • Making e-waste drop-off information accessible
  • Promoting sustainability in urban communities
  • Supporting local recycling initiatives

Our Features

  • Interactive map with real-time center availability
  • Information about working hours and accepted waste types
  • Emergency pickup and direct contact options
