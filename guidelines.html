E-Waste Disposal Guidelines

What to Dispose?

  • Mobile Phones, Laptops, Tablets
  • Chargers, Batteries, Headphones
  • Old Televisions, Refrigerators, Washing Machines
  • Printers, Scanners, Routers

Do Not Dispose

  • Wet waste or organic material
  • Glass shards or hazardous chemicals
  • Non-electronic items
  • Medical or biohazardous waste (unless specified)

Safety Tips

  • Erase all personal data before disposal
  • Do not dismantle electronics yourself
  • Use gloves while handling batteries
  • Transport e-waste in sealed containers

Why Dispose Properly?

  • Prevents harmful toxins from entering the environment
  • Allows recovery of valuable materials (e.g., gold, copper)
  • Reduces landfill waste and pollution
  • Supports green jobs and ethical recycling
